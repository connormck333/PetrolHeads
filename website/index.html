What are Petrol Heads?

Petrol Heads is a collection of 7,000 unique NFTs racing on the Ethereum Blockchain in single-seater ERC-721 race cars.

Owning a Petrol Head is an invitation to join a community of motorsport enthusiasts where you can participate in weekly motorsport-related giveaways and events.

Future utilities and perks will be unlocked by the community through roadmap activation.

Why join us?

Owning a Petrol Head not only presents you with a cool and unique profile picture, it grants access to a community filled with motorsport enthusiasts.

If you're crazy about motorsport, this is the club to join. We will host weekly giveaways and events all based around motorsport. Virtual Grands Prix and League races will be regular alongside a weekly giveaway which can range from a signed cap from a well-known driver to a ticket to your home F1 Grand Prix!

How can you join us?

To join our community, you must buy a Petrol Head NFT.

Petrol Heads have not yet been released; in the mean time, you can join us on our Discord channel to enter pre-release giveaways and be the first to be notified of updates.
